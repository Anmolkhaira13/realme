Breast Cancer Dataset Description


Citation

This breast cancer domain was obtained from the University Medical Centre, Institute of Oncology, Ljubljana, Yugoslavia. Thanks go to M. Zwitter and M. Soklic for providing the data.
This data set includes 201 instances of one class and 85 instances of another class. The instances are described by 9 attributes, some of which are linear and some are nominal.

Attribute Information

  1. Class: no-recurrence-events, recurrence-events.
  2. Age Group: 10-19, 20-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60-69, 70-79, 80-89, 90-99.
  3. Menopause: lt40 = less than 40 years , ge40 = greater than 40 , premeno = premature menopause.
  4. Tumor-size: 0-4, 5-9, 10-14, 15-19, 20-24, 25-29, 30-34, 35-39, 40-44,45-49, 50-54, 55-59.
  5. inv-nodes: 0-2, 3-5, 6-8, 9-11, 12-14, 15-17, 18-20, 21-23, 24-26,27-29, 30-32, 33-35, 36-39.
  6. Node-caps: yes, no.
  7. Deg-malig: Degree of malignancy range is : 1, 2, 3.
  8. Breast: left, right.
  9. breast-quad: left-up, left-low, right-up, right-low, central.
  10. Irradiat: Is there breast cancer? yes, no.

Other Information

Missing Attribute Values: denoted by "NaN"
Class Distribution:
1. no-recurrence-events: 201 instances.
2. recurrence-events: 85 instances
